Playfair Display and Fauna One

Introduction

Typography is an element of web development which can either make or break one's web page. It could be describe as a tool for communicating a text effectively to it's user. For today, I'm using Playfair Display and Fauna One provided by Google Fonts. Instead of going with what I feel will be great fonts, I followed the TypeConnection method, Embrace The Other.

How they embrace eachother?

Playfair, a late 18th century transitional design, is a serif font which is influenced from desings like Scotch Roman. John Baskverville, a transitional serif typeface designer, also influenced the desing. Playfair Display is great for headings and text which requires readers/users attention. On the other hand, Fauna One is a font which is used in paragraphs. With low contrast strokes and soft terminals, it embraces the bold design and sharpness of playfair display.
